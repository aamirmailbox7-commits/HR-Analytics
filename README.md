# HR People Analytics Power BI Dashboard



## Project Overview

Strategic workforce analysis of 311 employees designed to move beyond basic headcount reporting and uncover predictive retention risks and equity gaps.



## Key Findings



**Recruitment ROI Paradox**

Website hires show a **92.3% retention rate**, while Google Search hires drop to **38.8%**. Shifting budget toward direct portals could significantly reduce turnover costs.



**The "Missing Generation"**

A critical gap exists in the **25-34 age bracket** (only 5 employees) compared to the 35-44 bracket (150 employees), signaling a future leadership pipeline risk.



**Predictive Flight Risk**

Identified **11 high-performers** with perfect scores (4/4) who are at high risk of exiting due to low satisfaction and identified compensation gaps.



**Gender Pay Equity**

Data reveals a **$3,000 average salary gap** between male ($70.8K) and female ($67.8K) staff, providing a roadmap for data-driven equity adjustments.



## Tools Used

- Power BI

- DAX (Predictive logic)

- Excel (`HRDataset_v14.xlsx`)



## Dashboard Features

- **Retention Analysis**: Deep dive into hire source vs. long-term tenure.

- **Risk Mapping**: Geographic visualization of termination clusters.

- **Performance/Satisfaction Matrix**: Cross-filtered panels to identify high-value talent at risk.
